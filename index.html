Our Story

Crafting Elegance with Passion

Welcome to Breeze Couture, where fashion meets faith and style meets comfort. Founded by Oluwatosin in the heart of Isolo, Lagos, our brand has grown from a small tailoring service to a comprehensive fashion destination.

We understand that clothing is more than fabric—it's an expression of identity, faith, and personality. Whether you're looking for pristine white garments for CCC worship, sophisticated corporate wear, or trendy casual outfits, we bring your vision to life with meticulous attention to detail.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and ordering process.

You can place an order by contacting us via phone at 08169172711 or email at breezecouture95@gmail.com. We recommend visiting our store at Isolo, Lagos for precise measurements, especially for CCC white garments which require specific sizing for the complete set including head coverings.
Standard custom orders take 3-5 business days depending on the complexity of the design. CCC white garments and intricate traditional wears may take 5-7 business days. We also offer express services for urgent requests at a small additional fee. Ready-made items are available for immediate pickup.
Yes, we offer delivery services across Lagos and nationwide shipping to other states in Nigeria. Delivery fees vary based on location. Local deliveries within Isolo and surrounding areas are often free for orders above ₦20,000. We ensure all items are properly packaged to maintain their pristine condition during transit.
Absolutely! We offer free alterations within 7 days of pickup for any garment that doesn't fit as expected. Your satisfaction is our priority. For ready-made purchases, we provide alteration services at a minimal cost to ensure your new clothing fits you perfectly.
We specialize in both! You can purchase high-quality fabrics from us and have them tailored to your specifications, or choose from our extensive collection of ready-made wears. We stock a variety of materials including cotton, silk, lace, ankara, and specialized fabrics for white garments.
We accept cash payments at our store, bank transfers, and mobile money transfers. For custom orders, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion. We also offer flexible payment plans for large orders or bulk purchases for church groups and events.
